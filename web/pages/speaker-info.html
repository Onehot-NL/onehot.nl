Speaker information

Onehot is a community for people passionate about data science. We are centered in Utrecht, the Netherlands.

This page contains practical information for speakers. If you would like to speak at one of our events, please reach out! Email us at: organisation@onehot.nl

Audience

  • A healthy mix of data scientists, machine learning engineers, ops people, business analysts, academics, students, and other interersted folks.
  • Pretty technical, but no-one is an expert in every domain! If you are used to speaking for domain experts, please be prepared to give a little more context than usual.

About the talk

  • Talks can be about anything data-related that gratifies one's intellectual curiosity. If you would like examples, feel free to have a look at our previous meetups.
  • If you work for a company and you're hiring: feel free to mention this. However, please do not turn your talk into a "Why work for us"-pitch. It generally works better to focus on the content of the talk and show you are working on cool stuff anyway.
  • We are not too strict on time, but generally try to have two ~30 minute talks per meetup, excluding time for discussion/questions.

Coaching

  • We would like to be a safe space for new speakers to gain public speaking experience. We would love to help you brainstorm talk ideas beforehand, or coach you on your talk's narrative/slides/delivery.
