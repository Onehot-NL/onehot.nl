Code of Conduct

We want everyone to feel welcome and safe at our events. That's why we ask everybody to observe the following rules:

  1. Be excellent to each other.
  2. We absolutely do not allow discrimination or harassment on the basis of gender, race, sexual orientation, appearance, language, tabs vs. spaces, database of choice, CS vs IS, or anything else for that matter.
  3. Overly aggressive recruiting or marketing by anyone is not allowed.
  4. The general feel is "semi-professional-ish". Feel free to joke and have a tood time, but nothing dirty or off-colour please.

If you see or experience any issues at a Onehot event, please let us know and we'll take action right away. You can email us at info@onehot.nl or talk to one of our organizers at the event.
